# Privacy Policy

**Claim Chowder**
Last updated: February 20, 2026

## Overview

Claim Chowder ("the App") is a subscription and expense tracker for personal use. This policy explains what data we collect and how it is used.

## Data We Collect

When you use Claim Chowder, we collect:

- **Account information**: Your email address and password (used for authentication)
- **User-generated content**: Subscription and expense records you manually enter into the App (names, amounts, dates, currencies, notes)

We do not collect location data, contacts, photos, or any other device information.

## How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the App's core functionality:

- Authenticating your account
- Storing and displaying your subscription and expense records across devices

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

## Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using [Supabase](https://supabase.com), a cloud database provider. Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. You can review Supabase's privacy practices at [supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy).

## Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account exists. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us (see below).

## Children's Privacy

Claim Chowder is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL with an updated date.
