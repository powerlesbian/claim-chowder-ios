Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a subscription?

Tap the + button on the main list screen, fill in the details, and tap Save.

How do I delete my account?

Open the app, tap the person icon in the top-right corner, then tap Delete Account. You will be asked to confirm. This permanently deletes your account and all subscription data.

What currencies are supported?

Claim Chowder supports multiple currencies and normalises amounts to HKD for comparison purposes. You can enter any amount in the currency of your choice when adding a subscription.

How do I export my data?

Tap the document icon in the toolbar on the main list screen and choose Export CSV. This generates a CSV file you can save or share.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the sign-in screen, tap Forgot password? and enter your email address. You will receive a reset link.

Still need help?

Open a support request on GitHub Issues or email us directly. We aim to respond within 7 business days.
